# Dog Care Guidelines

## Walks
Adult dogs generally need 20-30 minutes of walking per session, one to two times
a day. High-energy breeds (Retrievers, Border Collies, working breeds) benefit
from 30-45 minutes. Walks are high priority for medium/large dogs since they
cover both exercise and bathroom needs.

## Feeding
Adult dogs eat 2 meals a day, roughly 10 minutes each including cleanup.
Puppies under 6 months may need 3 meals a day. Feeding is always high priority
- skipping it is not an option.

## Medication
Medication tasks (heartworm prevention, flea/tick treatment, prescribed meds)
are always high priority regardless of how quick the task is. Missing a dose
can have real health consequences. Duration is typically 5 minutes.

## Grooming
Bathing: 30-40 minutes, low-to-medium priority, roughly every 4-6 weeks for
most breeds (more often for dogs that get dirty/muddy).
Nail trimming: 10-15 minutes, medium priority, every 3-4 weeks.
Brushing: 10-15 minutes, low priority for short-haired breeds, medium for
long-haired/double-coated breeds, ideally daily during shedding season.

## Enrichment / Play
Fetch, tug, puzzle toys: 15-20 minutes, medium priority. Not essential daily
but improves behavior and reduces destructive tendencies if skipped for
long stretches.

## Vet & vaccinations
Annual checkups and core vaccinations (rabies, DHPP) are high priority but
infrequent (yearly) - schedule as a one-off task, not recurring daily/weekly.
